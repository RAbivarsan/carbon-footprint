## ✨ Features

### ⚡ Carbon Calculator
- Calculates CO₂ emissions based on:
  - 🚗 Travel distance  
  - ⚡ Electricity usage  
  - 🍖 Meat consumption  
- Instant feedback on environmental impact

---

### 🎯 Eco Challenges
- Daily sustainability tasks:
  - 🚶 Walk instead of driving  
  - 🛍️ Avoid plastic  
  - 💡 Save electricity  
- Click to complete and earn points

---

### 🏆 Gamification
- Earn rewards for eco-friendly actions  
- Encourages habit building  
- Makes sustainability fun  

---

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- Dynamic progress bar  
- Visual representation of improvement  

---

### 💾 Persistent Data
- Uses **localStorage**
- Saves progress even after page refresh
